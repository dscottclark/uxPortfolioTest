I am also a photographer

Since 2008, I’ve run my own freelance commercial lifestyle photography business and was quite happy with my career. I have been interested in studying UX and UI for years but couldn’t justify taking time away from my growing business.

Then came 2020, a weird year for everyone. I started the year full of inspiration and gusto for moving my business into larger markets and capitalizing on some big name clients I had shot projects for in 2019 - but then everything screeched to a halt, kicking off a tumultuous year that almost no-one expected.

With companies’ budgets frozen and no-one shooting in-person lifestyle photography, I thought, what better time to go to school! Now I have the skills to do two things that I love, and with my decade plus of experience working with companies and advertizing firms, I have a unique perspective and ability to combine those skills for designing killer UIUX products.
