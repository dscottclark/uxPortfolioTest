About D. Scott Clark

I am a creative UX/UI designer with a background in lifestyle & adventure photography. I think constantly about how to improve a user's experience within physical design and digital environments, everything from light switches to web apps. I've worked in the creative field for over a decade with brands like Starbucks, Jansport, & Chase Bank. My photography background allows me to design user experiences and interfaces that are intuitive and visually appealing even at early stages of the design process.
